Auckland is Electric. Let’s Keep It That Way.
Last season, Auckland FC brought the city together through unforgettable matchdays, record-breaking crowds and an atmosphere unlike anything New Zealand football had seen before. Together, supporters helped create something special - a club built on passion, energy and connection. However, one of the biggest challenges facing Auckland FC is maintaining supporter attendance once the excitement of a new club begins to fade. Great clubs are not built through one successful season alone; they are built through loyal supporters who continue showing up, season after season.
That is why Auckland FC is proud to launch “Blue Together - Bring Your Crew”, a new supporter initiative designed to encourage fans to stay connected beyond our inaugural season and keep the stadium atmosphere electric.
We understand that life gets busy and attending matches can sometimes become a “once in a while” activity. Whether it is work, study, family commitments or simply getting out of the habit, it can be easy to miss the next game. Our goal is simple: make Auckland FC matchdays something worth coming back to - with your mates, family or favourite football crew.

Who Is This For?
Blue Together has been designed for casual Auckland FC supporters aged 18–40 who attended one or more matches during our inaugural season but are not regular attendees or season members. Whether you came for the derby atmosphere, a fun social night out or to experience Auckland football for the very first time, we want to welcome you back and help turn one great matchday into many more unforgettable experiences.

What Are We Trying to Achieve?
Through Blue Together, Auckland FC aims to:
1. Increase repeat match attendance by encouraging casual supporters to return more regularly throughout the season.
2. Strengthen supporter connection by turning football into a shared social experience with friends, family and the wider Auckland FC community.
3. Maintain Auckland FC’s electric matchday atmosphere, supporting the long-term success of the club while continuing to create unforgettable experiences for fans.

How Does Blue Together Work?
It is simple - attend matches, bring your crew and unlock exclusive rewards.
MATCH 1: Bring a friend and receive 20% off your second ticket, making it easier to share the Auckland FC experience with someone new.
MATCH 2: Attend another match with family or friends and unlock an exclusive food and drink voucher to enjoy during matchday.
MATCH 3: Return for a third match and receive a limited-edition Auckland FC supporter reward, plus entry into an exclusive VIP matchday prize draw.

Supporters can also share their matchday moments online using #BlueTogether for the chance to win upgraded seats, exclusive fan experiences and additional Auckland FC prizes throughout the season.
At Auckland FC, football is about more than ninety minutes on the pitch - it is about belonging to something bigger. Together, we built the excitement of our inaugural season. Now, let’s build something lasting.
