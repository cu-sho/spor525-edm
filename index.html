Why This Initiative Matters

Keep the city coming back for more.

Last season, Auckland FC brought the city together through unforgettable matchdays, record-breaking crowds and an atmosphere unlike anything New Zealand football had seen before. Together, supporters helped create something special - a club built on passion, energy and connection.

However, Auckland FC's key challenge is keeping supporters engaged after the excitement of the first season fades. Great clubs are not built through one successful season alone; they are built through loyal supporters who continue showing up, season after season.

We understand that life gets busy, and matches can become a 'once in a while' activity. Work, study, family commitments or routine changes can make it easy to miss the next game. Our goal is simple: make Auckland FC matchdays something worth coming back to - with your mates, family or favourite football crew.

Blue Together in one line

Attend matches, bring your crew and unlock exclusive rewards that make returning to Auckland FC feel social, rewarding and unmissable.

The initiative is designed to turn the atmosphere people first came for into a habit they want to keep repeating all season.

Who Is This For?

Blue Together is designed for casual Auckland FC supporters aged 18-40 who attended at least one match last season but are not regular attendees or season members.

Whether you came for the derby atmosphere, a fun social night out or to experience Auckland football for the very first time, we want to welcome you back and help turn one great matchday into many more unforgettable experiences.

What Are We Trying to Achieve?

  1. Increase repeat match attendance by encouraging casual supporters to return more regularly throughout the season.
  2. Strengthen supporter connection by turning football into a shared social experience with friends, family and the wider Auckland FC community.
  3. Maintain Auckland FC's electric matchday atmosphere, supporting the long-term success of the club while continuing to create unforgettable experiences for fans.

How Does Blue Together Work?

It is simple - attend matches, bring your crew and unlock exclusive rewards.

Match 1

Bring a friend

Bring a friend and receive 20% off your second ticket, making it easier to share the Auckland FC experience with someone new.

Match 2

Come back with your crew

Attend another match with family or friends and unlock an exclusive food and drink voucher to enjoy during matchday.

Match 3

Unlock the VIP moment

Return for a third match and receive a limited-edition Auckland FC supporter reward, plus entry into an exclusive VIP matchday prize draw.

Matchday Sharing

Supporters can also share their matchday moments online using #BlueTogether for the chance to win upgraded seats, exclusive fan experiences and additional Auckland FC prizes throughout the season.

The Behavioural Objective

Make returning feel natural, social and worth planning.

This EDM reminds casual supporters of the atmosphere they loved and encourages them to plan another matchday with their crew.

At Auckland FC, football is around ninety minutes on the pitch. It is about belonging to something bigger. Together, we built the excitement of our inaugural season. Now, let's build something lasting.
